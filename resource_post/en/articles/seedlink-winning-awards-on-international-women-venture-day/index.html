Seedlink Winning Awards on International Women Venture Day
PostedWed 4, May 2016

The first entrepreneurs were the traders and merchants. They exchanged with one another for the various goods and services needed, increasing the benefits for all. As technological advancement today has drastically evolved the entrepreneurial climate, the value of the spirit to the modern society is even more important than ever. Entrepreneurship creates job opportunities and drives economic growth. More critically, entrepreneurship introduces new products and ideas to the market. Without the creative ideas of entrepreneurs our world might not be as advanced as it is today in the fields of culture, science and technology.

“Women Hold Up Half The Sky.”

The old Chinese saying is no less than true in entrepreneurial world. On April 27, the major female players of Shanghai’s startup ecosystem were brought together to celebrate entrepreneurship with Women Venture Day Shanghai at Jiao Tong University, Antai College of Economics and Management Campus. International Venture Days showcase the best of IE Business School’s entrepreneurial spirit. Such contest can connect startups with potential investors, and share experiences with each other, leading to the continued development and empowerment of local and international startup ecosystems.

The keynote speakers and panelists would discuss ways to advance the presence of women in startup projects. Selected women entrepreneurs will present their business elevator pitch in front of a jury comprised of a unique mix of industry experts, venture capitalists, business angels and investors. After rounds of selections, Our CCO(chief commercial officer), Rina Joosten-Rabou was one of the 10 finalists and was proudly invited to represent Seedlink in front of such a distinguished audience.

Rina presenting to panel members and audience about Seedlink’s technology and business model.

Female Entrepreneurs in China

To give a context as to why this event place emphasis on female entrepreneurship, we need to be aware that there are acute gender imbalance of participants specific to entrepreneurial activity, with a higher concentration of men in most sectors. While female entrepreneurship in China is higher than the global average, representing 41 percent of the Chinese private sector, women account for only 20% of the total entrepreneurs in China. Some of the primary challenges that deters women from venturing into entrepreneurship are lack of mentors, societal expectations and cultural upbringing, and a financing gap.

As such, prior to the competition, we had two rounds of panel discussion to tackle on these issues. The first discussion was about Shanghai’s startup ecosystem’s role in promoting female entrepreneurship, and the second focused on women’s role in advancing their own participation in entrepreneurship. The talks were lively and robust, and generated key insights such as to celebrate more female entrepreneurs in order to endorse more role models for women to look up to, and by offering more opportunities in industry it will give women the experience needed to pursue entrepreneurship.

Group photo with fellow contestants

Go, Seedlink! Go!

The pitching competition was intense yet immensely engaging, where all the female entrepreneurs gave captivating presentations on their startups and the tremendous value their respective services provide. The concepts ranged from an O2O gifting app which transforms digital gifts into real world experiences, to an online marketplace putting unique venues into creative uses – the diversity was wide and each was impressive in their own right. Against such strong competitors, Rina gave an impactful presentation, introducing Seedlink and our ability to index humanity, using artificial intelligence software to predict applicants’ job fit to an organisation within minutes.

We are extremely honoured to announce that Seedlink was awarded first place in the Women Venture Day Shanghai, and we would like to thank all the organisers for the opportunity to receive the privilege to showcase Seedlink on the prestigious platform.
